Introduction

Sponsor Sponsorson

Sponsor Sponsorson has a history of working to help those in need. Their latest project strives to bring the power of local merchants to anyone who may need their services, with the hope of creating a worldwide network.


The Project development team consists of two members: Backend Man and Tyler Johnson. By working closely with Sponsorson, the team has identified key features that could help fill a void in the market and expand the network globally.

With many years of experience, Sponsorson hopes to bring necessary services to others and expand the types of support that people in need can receive.


Their latest project hopes to achieve this by providing a simple tool that will allow users worldwide access to local merchants and their services, and help build relationships between them.

The Company

The company works to bridge the gap between merchants and those in need of services by using geolocating and simple search features.


Users can easily identify merchants close to them that provide the services they want, or find people that are able to provide their services in an online format that can be accessed from anywhere.


Additionally, through online payment handling, the company ensures that the payment process is simple for customers and that merchants are paid for their work so that they can continue to bring their services to others.

Website Demonstration

    Getting started is simple:

  1. An account is not needed to search for merchants, but for a connection and booking to be made you must first sign up.
  2. Following sign up, the search page will display merchants in your local area.
  3. You may use the filter dropdowns and range slider to set the search parameters you want. The map will adjust in real time and show markers for the merchants that match your search.
  4. To make a booking, simply click on the merchant’s calendar icon and choose an available date and time.
  5. The video has been replaced for the privacy of the owner. Enjoy the laughing frog!
